## Analysis Framework

For each party website, evaluate:

### Visual Design & Brand
- Brand colours and consistency
- Typography quality
- Photography and imagery
- Overall visual sophistication (1-5 rating)
- Emotional tone conveyed
- Distinctiveness from other parties

### Information Architecture
- Sitemap depth and breadth
- Navigation structure
- Policy content organisation
- News/blog structure
- About section depth
- Search functionality

### Content
- Policy page count and depth
- Blog/news frequency
- Content freshness (last updated)
- Content types (text, video, infographic, interactive)
- Storytelling vs. information approach
- Use of data and evidence

### Conversion & Engagement
- Join/membership pathway
- Donation flow
- Volunteer opportunities
- Email capture
- Social media links and integration
- Event listings

### SEO Performance
- Domain Rating (via Ahrefs)
- Organic keywords count
- Monthly organic traffic estimate
- Top ranking keywords
- Backlink profile quality
- Content gaps/opportunities

### Technical Performance
- Page speed (mobile/desktop)
- Core Web Vitals
- Mobile responsiveness
- Accessibility score
- SSL/security
- Platform/technology

### Te Reo Māori Integration
- Te Reo content availability
- Bilingual navigation
- Macron usage
- Cultural design elements
